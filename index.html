Sadra Zargarzadeh

I am a 2nd-year M.Sc. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alberta supervised by Prof. Mahdi Tavakoli in the Telerobotic and Biorobotic Systems Group. My research lies at the intersection of Robotics, Computer Vision, and AI with a focus on medical and surgical applications.

I was a recipient of the 2024 ETH Robotics Student Fellowship and served as a research fellow in the Soft Robotics Lab at ETH Zurich, and was awarded the E3 Fellowship at EPFL in 2023 at the Computational Robot Design & Fabrication Lab working on soft robotics. Previously, I earned my B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering at Sharif University of Technology.
